About Japanese Language

Japanese (日本語 – Nihongo) is spoken by more than 125 million people. It uses three scripts: Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji. Sentence structure generally follows Subject–Object–Verb order, and particles define grammatical roles.

Why Learn Japanese?

  • Understand anime, manga, and films
  • Travel confidently in Japan
  • Career opportunities in global companies
  • Improve cognitive and memory skills

Beginner Tips

  1. Master Hiragana and Katakana first
  2. Practice writing characters
  3. Use spaced repetition for vocabulary
  4. Read simple texts daily
